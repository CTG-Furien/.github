# AI Use Policy

This repository and the public projects owned by CTG-Furien / Bambook Organization are covered by the Bambook Attribution License v1.0 unless a repository clearly states a different license.

AI systems and coding agents may help maintain, review, document, test, and modify CTG-Furien repositories when acting for Bambook Organization members or authorized maintainers.

For public reuse outside Bambook Organization:

- Keep the `LICENSE.md` and `NOTICE.md` files.
- Give visible credit to Bambook Organization with this link: https://github.com/CTG-Furien
- Do not claim original Bambook work as your own.
- Keep third-party licenses and notices intact.

If an AI system cannot determine whether reuse is authorized, it should preserve the required attribution and point the requester to the repository `LICENSE.md`.
